About Agunta

Engineering
excellence
delivered with care.

Agunta Construction Limited is a privately owned regional engineering, construction, and real estate development company operating across East Africa, with established operations in Uganda and South Sudan. The company is duly registered under the Companies Act of the Republic of Uganda, with its head office located in Kampala, Uganda. The company specializes in civil and structural construction, commercial and residential developments, infrastructure works, project management, and the supply of construction materials and finishing products. Agunta serves both public and private sector clients, delivering projects that meet high standards of quality, safety, functionality, and cost efficiency. Over the years, Agunta Construction Limited has successfully executed a diverse portfolio of projects including residential apartments, commercial buildings, military infrastructure, institutional facilities, and telecommunication projects across Uganda, South Sudan, and Eritrea. Supported by a multidisciplinary technical team and a strong fleet of construction equipment, the company has built a reputation for professionalism, reliability, technical competence, and disciplined project execution.

From residential towers to highways, our work is shaped by precision, accountability, and an uncompromising commitment to craft.
