Our Story

Born from a bold African vision.

Agunta Construction Ltd was founded in 2007 in Kampala, Uganda with a single conviction: that Africa deserves world-class construction delivered by people who understand its soil, its people, and its potential.

What began as a regional contractor has grown into a multinational engineering force operating across Uganda, South Sudan, Ethiopia, Eritrea, and Ghana. Every project we undertake carries the weight of that founding belief.

"We don't just build structures — we build the infrastructure that allows communities to grow, economies to thrive, and futures to take shape."
— Founder, Agunta Construction Ltd

Today, our team of over 240 skilled engineers, architects, and construction professionals brings global standards to every African project — delivering results that speak for themselves.

How We Work

A Culture Built on Craft

At Agunta, we believe great construction begins long before the first brick is laid. It starts with people — their expertise, their integrity, and their shared commitment to doing things right.

We invest in our teams as heavily as we invest in our equipment. Every engineer, site manager, and tradesperson carries our reputation on their shoulders, and we equip them with the tools, training, and leadership to succeed.

Safety Above All

Zero-incident culture, weekly safety audits, and mandatory training for every person on site.

Continuous Learning

We fund certifications, send engineers to international conferences, and promote from within.

Community First

We hire locally wherever we operate, contributing to jobs, skills transfer, and economic growth.

Sustainable Practice

Green building standards, responsible material sourcing, and reduced environmental footprint.
